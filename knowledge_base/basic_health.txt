What is fever?
Fever is an increase in body temperature, often caused by infection or illness.

What is headache?
A headache is pain or discomfort in the head caused by stress, dehydration, lack of sleep, or illness.

What are symptoms of cold?
Common cold symptoms include sneezing, runny nose, sore throat, cough, and mild fever.

What is diabetes?
Diabetes is a condition in which blood sugar levels remain higher than normal.

What is blood pressure?
Blood pressure is the force of blood pushing against the walls of arteries as the heart pumps blood.
